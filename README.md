# Hi! I'm Emilio, a microbiologist interested in bioinformatics, transcriptomics, and computational biology.

I work with **RNA-seq and transcriptomic data**, and I'm interested in using bioinformatics to better understand molecular processes in biological systems, especially in non-model organisms.

My undergraduate thesis work at the **Instituto de Neurobiología (INB), UNAM** focused on de novo transcriptome assembly and differential expression analysis during regeneration in the terrestrial gastropod *Deroceras laeve*. Through this project, I strengthened my experience in transcriptomic workflows, omics data analysis, and reproducible computational approaches for biological research.

I’m also part of **ISCB RSG-Mexico**, where I participate in community-driven initiatives related to bioinformatics education, workshops, symposia, journal clubs, and outreach activities.

Currently, I continue expanding my skills in bioinformatics, computational biology, Python, and data analysis through research, teaching, training programs, and collaborative academic communities.
